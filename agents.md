Pokemon rules:

- Attach 1 energy per turn. 
- Attach energy by moving an energy card beneath a stack of pokemon cards.
- attach a tool by placing a Tool beneath a stack of pokmeon cards
- max 1 tool attached per pokemon card
- Evolve by playing stage 1 onto a basic, or a stage 2 onto a stage 1 pokemon
- cannot evolve the turn a pokemon was placed down
- When playing a supporter or item card, move it to 'staging'. When the card is done resolving, move it to discard.
- when you need room to do things (ie show cards to oponnent or re-arrange cards), you may wish to move cards to - staging temporarily
- retreat by first by moving the correct energy cards to discard. Then, move the stack of cards in active to an empty bench slot, or to staging. move the new active pokemon stack up from bench to active. if necessary, move old stack from staging to bench.
